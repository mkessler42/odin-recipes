Lasagna



Description

The best classic lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

Ricotta Mixture

  • 1lb/455g fresh ricotta (EASY recipe below or use nice store bought)
  • 75g or 3/4c grated parm
  • 100g or 1/2c heavy cream
  • 5g or 3/4tsp salt
  • 2g or 1tsp black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 1tsp nutmeg

Steps

  1. Layer ingredients into a 9x13 baking dish
  2. Cover the baking dish tightly with oiled piece of foil and bake in preheated 375F/190c oven for 25min.
  3. Remove foil and return to bake uncovered for 25min. Finish by broiling for 2-3 mins on high to brown the top. Allow to cool for 30 min before cutting and serving.
